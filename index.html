Biography

Portrait placeholder
Image coming soon

Born and raised in NYC I was imbued with a passion for architecture and design. Picking out the details amidst the inundation of monolithic buildings became a key element of my wanderings within the city.

At Hamilton College I am pursuing an art and economics degree, an intersection of the core elements of architecture, one driven by artistic pursuits and confined by economic concerns. My focus in art is sculpture, creating pieces that alter the space within which they stand, asking viewers to alter their bodily state to completely take in each detail.

Through economics I have learned to understand the limitations and proper practices within the business world.

I am curious and passionate about everything art and architecture related and would love to be part of an architecture team that is just as passionate.
